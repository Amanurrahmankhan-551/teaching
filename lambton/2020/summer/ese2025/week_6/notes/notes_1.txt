NANO is a built-in text editor for Linux

It is a "lightweight" program meaning that it does not take up much mass-storage space.

It is fairly intuitive to use. Here are some basic commands.

<CTRL><o> : saves your program
<CTRL><x> : exits from nano
<CTRL><k> : text cut
<CTRL><u> : paste text
